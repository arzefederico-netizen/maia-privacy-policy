Maia Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Maia is a personal AI assistant Alexa skill. This policy explains how the skill handles your data.

Data Collection

Maia does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Voice queries are sent to Anthropic's API solely to generate a response and are not retained after the session ends.

Third Party Services

This skill uses Anthropic's Claude API to process voice queries. Anthropic's privacy policy can be found at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

This skill does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the skill developer.
